Autumn Pumpkin Ravioli Bake

From @p  ·  Added 2026-04-06 11:30

A seasonal twist on classic comfort food, featuring roasted pumpkin and sage in a creamy baked ravioli dish. Perfect for cozy fall evenings!

👥 Serves 6 ⏱ Prep 20 minutes 🔥 Cook 35 minutes
ItalianPastaFallComfort FoodPumpkin

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 oz) package of fresh cheese ravioli
  • 1 small pumpkin, peeled and diced into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh sage, chopped
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh sage leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. 1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. 2. Toss the diced pumpkin with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, salt, and pepper on a baking sheet, and roast for 20-25 minutes or until tender.
  3. 3. In a large skillet, sauté the diced onion and minced garlic in the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil until softened.
  4. 4. Stir in the chopped sage and cook for an additional minute.
  5. 5. In a large bowl, combine the cooked ravioli, roasted pumpkin, and sage mixture.
  6. 6. In a separate bowl, mix together the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  7. 7. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with butter or cooking spray, then arrange half of the ravioli mixture in the dish.
  8. 8. Pour the cream mixture over the ravioli, then top with the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  9. 9. Repeat the layers, starting with the remaining ravioli mixture, then the cream mixture, and finally the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  10. 10. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown and bubbly.

Caramelized Fig and Prosciutto Flatbread

From @p  ·  Added 2026-04-06 11:31

Sweet and savory flatbread perfect for a quick dinner or appetizer, featuring caramelized figs and crispy prosciutto. A delicious combination of flavors and textures that will leave you wanting more.

👥 Serves 4 ⏱ Prep 15 minutes 🔥 Cook 20 minutes
ItalianFlatbreadAppetizerBrunchSweet and Savory

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup caramelized fig jam
  • 6 slices prosciutto
  • 1/4 cup fresh arugula
  • 1/2 cup shaved parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp balsamic glaze

Instructions

  1. 1. In a large bowl, combine warm water, olive oil, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until yeast is activated.
  2. 2. Add flour and salt to the bowl, mix until a dough forms.
  3. 3. Knead the dough for 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. 4. Roll out the dough into a 1/4 inch thick rectangle.
  5. 5. Spread the caramelized fig jam over the dough, leaving a 1 inch border around the edges.
  6. 6. Top the fig jam with prosciutto slices, arugula, and parmesan cheese.
  7. 7. Drizzle the balsamic glaze over the top.
  8. 8. Bake the flatbread in a preheated oven at 425°F for 15-20 minutes until crispy and golden brown.

Creamy Mushroom Chicken Fettuccine

From @p  ·  Added 2026-04-06 11:32

Indulge in a rich and creamy fettuccine dish loaded with sautéed mushrooms and tender chicken, perfect for a cozy night in. This recipe is a game-changer for pasta lovers!

👥 Serves 4 ⏱ Prep 15 minutes 🔥 Cook 20 minutes
ItalianPastaChickenMushroomsCreamyQuickComfort Food

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup mixed mushrooms (button, cremini, shiitake), sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. 1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. 2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-6 minutes.
  3. 3. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside. Add the sliced mushrooms to the skillet and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. 4. Add the minced garlic to the skillet and cook for 1 minute, until fragrant.
  5. 5. Pour in the heavy cream and bring the mixture to a simmer. Let cook for 2-3 minutes, until slightly thickened.
  6. 6. Stir in the Parmesan cheese, thyme, salt, and pepper. Let the sauce simmer for an additional 2 minutes, until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.
  7. 7. Add the cooked fettuccine pasta to the skillet, tossing to coat with the creamy mushroom sauce.
  8. 8. Return the chicken to the skillet and toss everything together to combine.
  9. 9. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley if desired.

Creamy Truffle Mushroom Pasta Bake

From @p  ·  Added 2026-04-06 11:35

Indulge in a rich and creamy pasta bake loaded with sautéed mushrooms and truffle flavor, perfect for a cozy night in. This comforting dish is sure to become a favorite!

👥 Serves 6 ⏱ Prep 20 minutes 🔥 Cook 35 minutes
ItalianPastaComfort FoodMushroomTruffleCreamyBaked

Ingredients

  • 12 oz pasta of choice
  • 2 cups mixed mushrooms (button, cremini, shiitake)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp truffle oil
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. 1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. 2. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente, then set aside.
  3. 3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat and sauté the chopped onion until translucent.
  4. 4. Add the mixed mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown.
  5. 5. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  6. 6. In a large saucepan, whisk together the heavy cream, flour, salt, pepper, and dried thyme.
  7. 7. Bring the cream mixture to a simmer and cook until it thickens, stirring constantly.
  8. 8. Remove the cream sauce from the heat and stir in the mozzarella and parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  9. 9. Add the cooked pasta, sautéed mushroom mixture, and truffle oil to the cream sauce, stirring until well combined.
  10. 10. Transfer the pasta mixture to a baking dish and top with additional grated cheese.
  11. 11. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown and bubbly.
